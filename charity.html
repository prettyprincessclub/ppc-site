This post is aimed at people who want to fight racial injustice in their communities but don't know how to start. Image provided by @prettyprincess.club
* Get involved in local politics: Is your city council voting to defund the police or fund community aid efforts? Find the city councilmember for your district and call, email, or snail-mail them to encourage them to pledge their support.
* Donate Money: To organizations like the Equal Justice Initiative or directly to people affected by injustice.
* Donate Goods: Your local mutual aid organization might have a list of specific goods they need to support their community. Check their current needs, mask up, and go shopping!
* Educate Your Friends and Family: Find ways to discuss your role in combating racial injustice with your people every day.
