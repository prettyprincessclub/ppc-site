Our Fashion Shows

We are proud to host two fashion shows at Royal Vegas Retreat 2021: one for all things J-fashion, and one dedicated to Lolita fashion! Model applications are open until October 19 on Fluffy Show. Please keep in mind that all models must have a GA or VIP ticket to Royal Vegas Retreat. Read on to find our more about each show.

All Things J-Fashion

Friday, November 19

Our All Things J-Fashion show will feature the following brands:

  • Fluffy Tori
  • GHOST GiRL GOODS
  • KittyKaya
  • Kuroshiro Kawaii
  • Puvithel
  • The Black Ribbon

Models can apply to model in this show or both shows on Fluffy Show until October 19.

Lolita Fashion

Saturday, November 20

Our Lolita Fashion show will feature the following brands:

  • Enchanted Dream Couture
  • Flores Astorum
  • Fluffy Tori
  • KittyKaya
  • Kuroshiro Kawaii
  • Puvithel
  • The Black Ribbon

Models can apply to model in this show or both shows on Fluffy Show until October 19.

How Do I Apply?

If you're interested in modeling, you can apply NOW on Fluffy Show. Here's how it works:

  1. Create an account on Fluffy Show
  2. Create a model profile - this might take a little while because your profile will have a lot of information in it! Just remember that you can use this profile to apply to any other show that uses Fluffy Show, so you only have to enter all of this information one time.
  3. Apply to the shows you're interested in! For Royal Vegas Retreat, you can apply to either or both of our shows.
  4. After the October 19 deadline, our team will close applications and work with brands to select models. We need all the models we can get to put on an amazing show, so please apply!

What to Expect

Modeling is an amazing experience that puts you in contact with designers, brand owners, and other fashion fans. It requires a bit of a time commitment, but don't forget that our main stage panels will be streamed live on Twitch and the VODs for both the main stage panels and community panels will be posted to the JFashion on Demand YouTube channel after the event. Model call time is 3 hours before the show on both days. We may ask you to attend a brief rehearsal in the morning before our content starts. We have staff and volunteers on hand to organize and assist you, but please be prepared to do your own hair and makeup. We may ask you to model for more than one brand in a show. Designers and brands may also ask you to bring wardrobe staples like shoes, wigs, and petticoats to assist them in creating the perfect look.

Seeing Lolita and J-fashion looks on a runway in person is such an important part of our event, and that's why we need your help - thank you in advance for signing up to model!

If you have any questions, feel free to drop us a line: info@prettyprincess.club
