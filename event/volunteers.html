Volunteer at Royal Vegas Retreat

We need your help to make this event as fabulous as possible!

Sign up to volunteer at Royal Vegas Retreat 2021 now: https://forms.gle/eeqKQQVkdQtdBKLs7

We will accept volunteer applications on a rolling basis, so sign up early, and tell your friends!

Volunteers can sign up to assist in one of many areas, including:

  • Registration
  • Fashion show coordination (separate from modeling - we will release model applications in late 2021)
  • Remote vending
  • VIP guest experience
  • Advertising and outreach in your area
  • ...and more!

We are thrilled to offer our volunteers free general admission tickets in exchange for their work. Volunteers will be responsible for their own travel and accommodations, and must complete at least 8 hours of work to qualify.

If we accept your application, we will reach out to you with more details about your commitment and which domain you will work in.

Thank you so much! We can't wait to work with you at Royal Vegas Retreat 2021.
