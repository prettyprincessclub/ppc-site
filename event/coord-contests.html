Sign up to enter our coord contests by November 14!

Our Coord Contests

Are you interested in entering either of our Lolita fashion coord contests? Registration is open from October 12 to November 14. You can sign up using our Google form. Please keep in mind that all entrants must have a GA or VIP ticket to Royal Vegas Retreat 2021. We will also announce prizes and judges for both contests as we get closer to the event. Read on for more details about the Lolita Fashion Coord Contest and the Handmade Lolita Fashion Coord Contest, and stay tuned for updates!

Lolita Fashion Coord Contest

For any coordinate where the outfit is not primarily handmade by the entrant, this is the contest for you! On Friday, November 19, fashion enthusiasts will show off their coords in front of our judges and audience for the chance to win fabulous prizes donated by our vendors and sponsors. Coords must be Lolita fashion or a sibling style such as ouji or EGA, or may choose to fuse elements of other J-fashion styles into a Lolita fashion coordinate. We hope to see your creativity and imagination come to life on the stage!

Handmade Lolita Fashion Coord Contest

The Handmade Lolita Fashion Coord Contest is for any coordinate where 100% of the main clothing items (blouse, dress, pants, vest, jacket, etc.) are handmade by the entrant. The entrant may opt to have another person wear the coordinate for the purposes of judging and display. On Saturday, November 20, fashion enthusiasts will show off their coords in front of our judges and audience for the chance to win fabulous prizes donated by our vendors and sponsors. Coords must be Lolita fashion or a sibling style such as ouji or EGA, or may choose to fuse elements of other J-fashion styles into a Lolita fashion coordinate. We hope to see your creativity and imagination come to life on the stage!
