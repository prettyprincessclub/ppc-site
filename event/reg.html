RVR Registration

Ticket reservations will open on June 26th at 12:00 PM PT. At this time, you will be able to reserve your GA or VIP ticket on our Eventbrite page. (Our Eventbrite page still shows us as postponed right now - don't worry! We will be making updates behind the scenes to prepare for ticket sales.)

GA tickets will cost $5 to reserve. VIP tickets will cost $20 to reserve. Only GA and VIP tickets will be available during the reservation period.

The reservation period ends July 31st or when we sell out of tickets.

On August 14th we will ask everyone who reserved a ticket during the reservation period to pay the remainder of their ticket price. At this time, you will pay the remaining $120 for GA tickets and $180 for VIP tickets.

In addition, if any tickets remain after the reservation period, or if we are able to increase our attendance cap, we will offer GA, VIP, and Limited-Access Partner Pass tickets at full price. Prices are as follows:

  • GA: $125
  • VIP: $200
  • Partner Pass: $20

We are no longer offering Sunday-only tickets. The Partner Pass is a special ticket type for partners of attendees who do not wear J-fashion. Partner Pass ticketholders are not required to adhere to the dress code. However, they may only access the vendors' area and fashion show content.

We are proud to announce that we have an official room block with the MGM Grand with an excellent event rate. Please visit our Room Block and Venue page for more information and to make your reservation with the MGM Grand now!
