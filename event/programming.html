Programming

RVR Programming

We're planning an incredible event stacked with fashion shows, a tea party, games, panels, and shopping.

We will release more information about official event programming as we get closer to the event, including special guest panels and VIP events.

Community panel applications are now closed. We will notify prospective panelists of acceptance, rejection, or waitlist status by October 9.

Coord contest entries will open starting October 12. See our Coord Contests page for more info.

Interested in casual excursions around the strip? Join our Facebook group to help us plan!

Check out our tentative schedule below - subject to change prior to event.

Before the Event

VIP Badge Pickup

Calling all VIPs! Arrive Thursday, the day before the main event starts, to pick up your badge before everyone else. Get settled in and start the party!

VIP Perk: Early badge pickup is open to VIPs, special guests, and staff only.

Day 1

Friday, November 19 2021

VIP Mixer

Calling all VIPs! Get your pre-funk on bright and early on Friday for an exclusive mixer (with alcoholic beverages available for purchase for those who are 21+). Meet your fellow VIPs and get some early shopping time in!

VIP Perk: This event is open to VIPs, special guests, and staff only.

Shopping

All day long, enjoy browsing our vendors' room for apparel, accessories, jewelry, novelties, and art from our talented vendors.

Fashion Shows

On Friday and Saturday afternoon, our brands will show off their collections on the runway. See their looks - then head to the vendors' room to buy them!

VIP Perk: VIPs will have priority seating at this event.

Coord Contest - Lolita Fashion General

Love to compete? Put your coording skills to the test. All Lolita fashion and Lolita-adjacent (i.e. Ouji, EGA, fusion with other J-fashion, etc.) coordinates welcome. See our Coord Contests page for more information.

Judges and prizes to be announced.

VIP Perk: VIPs will have priority seating at this event.

Special Guest Panels

More info coming soon! For now, see our special guest lineup and get hype.

VIP Perk: VIPs will have priority seating at all special guest panels.

Community Panels

Attendees will present panels on a variety of Lolita and J-fashion topics. Stay tuned for more info!

Open Mic Night

Bring your talents, skits, comedy, and more! Alcoholic beverages will be available for purchase for those who are 21+. See our open mic night page for details.

Saturday, November 20 2021

Shopping

All day long, enjoy browsing our vendors' room for apparel, accessories, jewelry, novelties, and art from our talented vendors.

Swap Meet

Support the secondhand market! Shop 'til you drop or sign up to sell starting September 6. See our Swap Meet page for more details.

Fashion Shows

On Friday and Saturday afternoon, our brands will show off their collections on the runway. See their looks - then head to the vendors' room to buy them!

VIP Perk: VIPs will have priority seating at this event.

Coord Contest - Lolita Fashion Handmade

Are you a talented sewist who regularly makes their own coords? Wear your best handmade outfit - by which we mean all main pieces (dresses, blouses, skirts, trousers, vests, etc.) must be handmade by you! Entrants are not required to make their own shoes, socks, accessories, or jewelry, but we strongly encourage you to show off as much of your handiwork as possible! See our Coord Contests page for more information.

Judges and prizes to be announced.

VIP Perk: VIPs will have priority seating at this event.

Community Panels

Attendees will present panels on a variety of Lolita and J-fashion topics. Stay tuned for more info!

Movie Screening

Cuddle up with a Lolita fashion movie classic and an adult beverage (for those 21+) to wind down your Saturday night.

Day 3

Sunday, November 21 2021

Shopping

Enjoy browsing our vendors' room for apparel, accessories, jewelry, novelties, and art from our talented vendors.

Special Guest Panels

More info coming soon! For now, see our special guest lineup and get hype.

Tea Party

The highlight of any Lolita fashion event: the tea party! Included with every GA and VIP ticket, the tea party will be an elegant affair, with pastries, desserts, savory eats, and of course - tea!

VIP Perk: VIPs will have priority seating at this event.

VIP/Staff Afterparty

Not ready for the weekend to end? For VIPs and staff, we have the solution: join us at our afterparty! Wind down with us at this exclusive event.

VIP Perk: This event is open to VIPs, special guests, staff, and volunteers only.
