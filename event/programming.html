Programming

RVR Programming

We're planning an incredible event stacked with fashion shows, a tea party, games, panels, and shopping.

We will release more information about official event programming as we get closer to the event, including special guest panels and VIP events.

Interested in presenting a panel at Royal Vegas Retreat? Fill out our community panelist application by October 1. We will notify prospective panelists of acceptance, rejection, or waitlist status by October 9.

Applications for models and coordinate contest entrants will open in September and October. Please stay tuned for more details!

Interested in casual excursions around the strip? Join our Facebook group to help us plan!

Check out our tentative schedule below - subject to change prior to event.

Before the Event

VIP Badge Pickup

Calling all VIPs! Arrive Thursday, the day before the main event starts, to pick up your badge before everyone else. Get settled in and start the party!

VIP Perk: Early badge pickup is open to VIPs, special guests, and staff only.

Day 1

Friday, November 19 2021

VIP Mixer

Calling all VIPs! Get your pre-funk on bright and early on Friday for an exclusive mixer. Meet your fellow VIPs and get some early shopping time in!

VIP Perk: This event is open to VIPs, special guests, and staff only.

Shopping

All day long, enjoy browsing our vendors' room for apparel, accessories, jewelry, novelties, and art from our talented vendors.

Fashion Shows

On Friday and Saturday afternoon, our brands will show off their collections on the runway. See their looks - then head to the vendors' room to buy them!

Interested in modeling in our fashion show? Create a profile on Fluffy Show! On September 14, we will open our shows to applications on Fluffy Show. Application deadline: October 19

VIP Perk: VIPs will have priority seating at this event.

Coord Contest - Lolita Fashion General

Love to compete? Put your coording skills to the test. All Lolita fashion and Lolita-adjacent (i.e. Ouji, EGA, fusion with other J-fashion, etc.) coordinates welcome.

Judges and prizes to be announced.

VIP Perk: VIPs will have priority seating at this event.
