Royal Vegas Online Tea Party

Thank you for your interest in participating in our RVO Virtual Tea Party! The tea party will be held on November 15th, 2020, at 2:00 PM PT.

We will host our tea party virtually on the Bay Area Kei Discord server. Tables will consist of small video chat rooms with 6 or 7 guests each, along with a designated host to moderate. Potential tea party activities include fun roundtable questions to 'break the ice', party games, and of course, free time to chat with your tablemates. The JFashion On Demand team will also pop into the table rooms and play games on stream with rooms that have opted in to be featured.

This Virtual Tea Party is a closed, limited-seating event. Attendance will be capped at 8 rooms to maintain a quality experience. If you RSVP and do NOT receive a confirmation email, we have reached seating capacity and will automatically add you to our waitlist. RSVPs will close on November 10 OR when we hit capacity, whichever is sooner.

To register for this event, you must submit proof of donation of $5 or more to the Equal Justice Initiative, our chosen beneficiary for this event. (This donation will be non-transferable/non-refundable. You may donate as much as you want, but we will only issue one entry to the Charity Drawing Contest for any donated amount.)

As part of your tea party registration, you will receive one (1) entry to our Charity Drawing Contest, and are eligible to win prizes from our participating brands.

To register starting October 10 at 12:00 PM PT, please fill out our Tea Party Signup Form. If the Tea Party Signup Form is not taking responses after October 10 at 12:00 PM PT, we have reached capacity. Please fill out the Tea Party WAITLIST Form instead.
