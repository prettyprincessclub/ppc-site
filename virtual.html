Charity Drawing

We will hold a charity drawing to benefit the Equal Justice Initiative. We will announce the winners at the Royal Vegas Online closing ceremonies. You must submit your entries by November 15 at 2:00 PM PT.

No monetary purchase is necessary to enter or win the drawing. Please note that the drawing is only open to United States residents. It is not open to residents of Alabama, Hawaii, Kansas, New Jersey, and Utah, in compliance with applicable state laws.

You can enter our drawing by 1. Registering for the Tea Party or Tea Party waitlist, 2. Submitting proof of donation of $5 or more to the Equal Justice Initiative, or 3. reposting our informational Instagram post (see this page for more information). Limit one entry per person.

We will choose winners by random drawing, and each winner will receive one of the following: a $125 gift card from BB & B, a Fluffy Tori bundle (pin of your choice, sticker sheet, coord planner notepad), a prize from Twootie Tarte to be announced, or a PPC Merch Shop swag bundle.

Fashion Walk and Coord Contest

Submissions for our Fashion Walk and Coord Contest open on October 10 and close on November 3. To apply to the Coord Contest, simply opt in on the Fashion Walk submission form. Please submit only one entry to the Coord Contest. You may submit up to 2 entries for the Fashion Walk. While we encourage you to get inspired by our theme "Royalty: In Your Castle," you are not required to adhere to it.

Coords should fit into the genre of Lolita fashion, Ouji fashion, EGA, or a fusion of Lolita and another J-Fashion.

In addition: You MUST be 18 years or older to participate. Outfits should not be sexually explicit. NO cosplay or costumes, and NO replicas.

We will award a prize to the 2 Coord Contest winners determined by popular vote and by a panel of judges consisting of the JFashion On Demand team and Nif from Pretty Princess Club. Deadline for votes is November 15 at 2:00 PM PT. We can't wait to see your wonderful entries!

The winner of the Popular Vote Prize will receive a Fluffy Tori bundle (pin of your choice, sticker sheet, coord planner notepad), a gift card from GHOST GiRL GOODS, and a PPC Merch Shop swag bundle. The winner of the Judges' Panel Prize will receive a a Fluffy Tori bundle (pin of your choice, sticker sheet, coord planner notepad), a promo code from Tour New Soul, and a PPC Merch Shop swag bundle.

FAQ

What's the Theme of Royal Vegas Online?

The theme is Royalty: In Your Castle. We're still suffering the effects of the global pandemic, and for many of us, that means we're confined to our castles with the exception of essential work and errands. How do you make yourself feel Royal? For your fashion walk/coord contest submissions and tea party coords, we encourage you to draw from the themes of royalty and nobility throughout history, and from common motifs we love in Lolita Fashion, like crowns, scepters, jewels, and thrones. Of course, our theme is just a prompt - you are not required to adhere to it at any time.

We will also post inspiring Instagram prompts starting in November. How do you make your castle inviting, cozy, and aesthetic, and what do you wear to make you feel your best? Have you perfected the perfect afternoon tea to eat at home, before, during, or after a virtual tea party? We want to know - and we'll be sharing a random selection of submissions.

How do I sign up?

You can join our Facebook event.

For the Fashion Walk, Coord Contest, Pub Trivia, and Tea Party, you will need to sign up via our intake forms. All forms open October 10 and close November 3, so please mark your calendars now! We will post the forms here and on social media when they are avaiable.

How do I buy a ticket to Royal Vegas Online?

RVO is free to watch and enjoy via Twitch and Discord. However, we will require a small donation to a charity to ensure your spot at the tea party.

How does this affect the in-person Royal Vegas Retreat event?

Tl;dr: it doesn't. We are still hoping to hold our flagship in-person event Royal Vegas Retreat in November 2021.
